Top Disney Night Dances

Here are the top 10 unforgettable Disney Night dances on Dancing with the Stars that capture the magic, drama, and charm of Disney like never before. From Riker Lynch’s fierce Pirates of the Caribbean Paso Doble to Simone Biles’ powerful Moana Contemporary, these routines blend storytelling, choreography, and jaw-dropping performances that left viewers spellbound. Dive into this list to relive the spectacular nights where contestants truly embodied Disney heroes, from Milo Manheim’s superhero Quickstep as Mr. Incredible to Zendaya’s elegant waltz as Belle from Beauty and the Beast. Discover what made each dance stand out in a night of pure Disney enchantment!

1. Riker Lynch and Allison Holker – Pirates of the Caribbean – Paso Doble (Season 20)

This dance was explosive, capturing the intensity and drama of Pirates of the Caribbean. Riker embodied Jack Sparrow’s swagger and confidence, while Allison’s choreography was sharp, bold, and exciting, making this Paso Doble a fan favorite.

2. Simone Biles and Sasha Farber – Moana – Contemporary (Season 24)

Simone’s athleticism and grace shone in this performance. The dance captured Moana’s adventurous spirit and strength, with Simone perfectly embodying the character’s journey. The contemporary style allowed for fluidity and raw emotion that made it especially moving.

3. Jordan Fisher and Lindsey Arnold – Moana – Foxtrot (Season 25)

Jordan’s Foxtrot was seamless and sophisticated. It combined Disney magic with technical excellence, and his natural chemistry with Lindsey made the dance feel as effortless and enchanting as the movie itself.

4. Milo Manheim & Witney Carson – The Incredibles – Quickstep (Season 27)

This Quickstep was energetic, fun, and full of character, embodying The Incredibles’ family dynamics and superhero theme. Milo’s enthusiasm and Witney’s inventive choreography made this dance unforgettable and full of personality.

5. Illona Maher and Alan Bersten - Encanto - Jazz (Season 33)

This dance was a vibrant, joyful celebration of Encanto, with Illona’s energy and Alan’s inventive choreography blending seamlessly to bring the music to life in a way that captured the movie's spirit and made the performance a fan favorite.

6. Normani Kordei and Val Chmerkovskiy – Mulan – Paso Doble (Season 24)

Normani embodied Mulan’s warrior spirit with fierce intensity. The dramatic storytelling and precision made this performance unforgettable.

7. Zendaya and Val Chmerkovskiy – Beauty and the Beast – Viennese Waltz (Season 16)

Zendaya’s waltz captured the romance and elegance of *Beauty and the Beast*. Her graceful performance brought the classic Disney story to life, earning praise for its beauty and fluidity.

8. Charli D’Amelio and Mark Ballas – The Simpsons (Disney+) – Jazz (Season 31)

Although unconventional, Charli and Mark’s jazz number brilliantly captured the quirky essence of the Simpsons. Mark’s creativity and Charli’s skill made this performance uniquely captivating.

9. Nyle DiMarco and Peta Murgatroyd – Tarzan – Samba (Season 22)

Nyle’s samba embodied Tarzan’s wild, free-spirited nature. His connection with Peta and his ability to perform with grace and rhythm (despite being deaf) made this dance both powerful and inspiring.

10. Nastia Liukin and Derek Hough – Frozen – Jazz (Season 20)

This energetic Jazz routine brought a fresh, playful twist to Frozen's iconic song, with Nastia’s precision and Derek’s creative choreography perfectly capturing the fun, whimsical chemistry of Anna and Hans, making it a standout performance of the season.
